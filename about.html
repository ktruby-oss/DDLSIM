About the Project Lead

Kaitlyn Brishae Truby

Kaitlyn Brishae Truby is the lead researcher and creator behind DDLSim-Lab. With a focus on distributed systems, AI infrastructure, and network fault tolerance, her work aims to bridge the gap between theoretical AI models and real-world system deployments.

Contact: kaitlyn.truby@student.uagc.edu

Call for Infrastructure Partners

DDLSim-Lab is a fully open-source research platform available to any researcher, professor, or student worldwide. To advance this project and enable larger-scale experiments, we are actively seeking contributions of:

  • Bare-metal servers
  • Cloud computing credits
  • Advanced networking hardware

Your support directly accelerates AI systems research and helps the global scientific community. We welcome partnerships with testbeds, universities, and technology providers.
